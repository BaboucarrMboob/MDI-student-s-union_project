The Student's Union is a body established to aspire for academic excellent, support student's initiatives and promote values of an open society, service leadership and democracy. It also endeavors to prevent discrimination on any basis and any form and to enable fruitful collaboration within the institute and outside of it, and to mould students for a fruitful and an inspiring future. The union also makes strong ties among it's members and the administration and other relevant stakeholders to achieve its common goal towards national development.
